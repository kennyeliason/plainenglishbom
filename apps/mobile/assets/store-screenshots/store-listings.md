# App Store Listings

## English

**App name:** Plain English Book of Mormon

**Short description (80 char max):**
Read the Book of Mormon in clear, modern English. Same message, easier to read.

**Full description:**
Experience the Book of Mormon like never before — translated into clear, modern English while preserving its original meaning and spiritual power.

WHAT IS THIS APP?

Plain English Book of Mormon takes the sacred text and converts archaic King James-style language into contemporary English that's easier to understand. Words like "thee," "thou," "hath," and "wherefore" become natural, modern phrases — making the scriptures more accessible without changing their message.

FEATURES

• Complete Book of Mormon text in modern English
• Side-by-side comparison with original text
• Clean, distraction-free reading experience
• Dark mode for comfortable reading
• Offline access — no internet required
• Track your reading progress
• Available in English and Spanish

WHO IS THIS FOR?

• New readers exploring the Book of Mormon for the first time
• Anyone who finds older English difficult to understand
• Teachers and parents helping others study the scriptures
• Long-time readers wanting fresh perspective on familiar passages

OUR APPROACH

We've carefully modernized the language while staying faithful to the original text. This isn't a paraphrase or interpretation — it's the same Book of Mormon you know, just easier to read.

PRIVACY FOCUSED

• No account required
• No ads
• Minimal data collection (anonymous analytics only)
• Your reading stays on your device

Start reading today and discover the Book of Mormon in language that speaks to you.
